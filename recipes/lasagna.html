Homemade Lasagna

An easy to make recipe of a delicious lasagna

This lasagna recipe has in mind people with a limited amount of time that want a good and beautiful dish for a Sunday lunch with family or friends.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Tomato sauce
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Lasagna dough
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Cook bolognesa sauce
  2. Put lasagna in water
  3. create layers of lasagna dough and sauce
  4. Put it in the oven
  5. Bon appetit!
