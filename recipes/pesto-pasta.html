Pasta with Pesto

An easy to make recipe of a homemade pesto with pasta

This is a good recipe for a Sunday with friends. By making pesto from scratch, we're creating a beautiful and tasty dish.

Ingredients

  • Basil
  • Garlic
  • Nuts
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Smash the basil leaves with garlic, salt and the nuts
  2. Add olive oil
  3. Cook pasta
  4. Put a bit of pasta water into pesto sauce
  5. Mix pasta and pesto
